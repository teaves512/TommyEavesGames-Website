Education

Staffordshire University

Currently, I am studying at Stafforshire University under the award BSc (Hons) Computer Games Programming, with the intention of going on a placement year, starting September 2022.

During my first year, I have developed a particular interest in Artificial Intelligence and Graphical Programming within games.



Blackpool Sixth Form College

From September 2018 to July 2020, I studied at Blackpool Sixth Form College. I achieved A* A* A grades, in the following subjects:

  • A Level Mathematics | A*
  • A Level Graphic Design | A*
  • A Level Physics | A

In the summer of 2018, I earned 10 GCSEs, including Mathematics and English.

Skills

Programming

I have over 4 years of experience with using C# and Python. I am currently learning C++, HTML and CSS, during my studies at Stafforshire University. Go and follow me on Stack Overflow for more!

Game Development

Primarily, I have used Unity to develop games for just over 3 years. You can play my games over on Itch so make sure to give me a follow! I have also experimented with other game engines, including Unreal Engine 4, Game Maker Studio 2 and RPG Maker MV.

Graphic Design

Graphic Design is another passion of mine. Over the past 5 years, I have worked on many different briefs, producing anything from logos, to posters, to restaurant menus and even CD covers. I am a member of the Adobe Creative Cloud and often use the following applications:

  • Adobe Photoshop CC
  • Adobe Illustrator CC
  • Adobe InDesign CC
